## Do’s

1 Think before you post—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate

2 Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information

3 Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation

4 Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication (2FA)

5 Maintain a positive digital footprint that reflects well on your personal and professional image

## Don’ts

1 Don’t share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords)

2 Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or online arguments

3 Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content

4 Don’t click on suspicious links or messages from unknown sources

5 Don’t spend excessive time on social media at the cost of studies or health
