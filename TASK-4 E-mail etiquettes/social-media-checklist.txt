## Do’s

1. Protect your details by changing your account settings and keeping private info to yourself.

2. Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading information.

3. Use passwords and turn on two-factor authentication.

4. Keep your presence positive and good for your personal and work life.


## Don’ts

1. Don't share info like your address, phone number or passwords.

2. Don't be mean or bully others online. Avoid getting into arguments.

3. Don't post content that's not suitable or is hurtful.

4. Don't click on links or messages, from people you don't know.

5. Don't spend much time on social media. It can affect your studies and health.
