## Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

* Always check the emails, messages, and/or link before clicking. Be extra cautious while clicking messages that require you to do something

* Avoid using Wi-Fi for activities like banking and logging in to accounts.

* Keep your phone and laptop updated with the latest security patches and antivirus programs.

* Be cautious while sharing media. Avoid sharing details like college name, address, and phone number.

* Only download apps from stores like Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

* Do not share your UPI PIN, OTP, and banking details with anyone. This includes bank officials too.

* Always be cautious while making payments. Double-check the receiver's UPI ID and/or QR code before making a payment.

* Do not accept payment requests from apps like Google Pay and PhonePe.

* Enable transaction alerts. Check bank statements regularly for suspicious transactions.

* If you think you have become a victim of cyber fraud, report it to the National Cyber Crime Portal cybercrime.gov.in.

* Call the Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930 and report the incident as soon as possible to stop financial fraud.

* If you think your account is in danger, report it to your bank as soon as possible. They can stop the transaction. Freeze your account if needed.
