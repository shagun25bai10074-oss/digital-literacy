# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

1 Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible

2 Always verify emails, messages, or links before clicking—especially those asking for urgent action

3 Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities like banking or logging into important accounts

4 Keep your phone and laptop updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software

5 Limit the personal information you share on social media (college, address, phone number, etc.)

6 Download apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store

7 Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone—even if they claim to be from your bank

8 Always double-check the receiver’s UPI ID or QR code before making any payment

9 Avoid accepting unknown “collect request” (payment requests) on apps like Google Pay or PhonePe

10 Enable transaction alerts and regularly check your bank statements for any suspicious activity

11 Immediately report cyber fraud on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in)

12 Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 as soon as possible to report financial fraud

13 Inform your bank quickly to block transactions or freeze your account if needed
