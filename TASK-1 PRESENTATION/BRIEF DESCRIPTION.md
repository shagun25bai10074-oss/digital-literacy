## BRIEF DESCRIPTION

The poster has been created using the Canva tool by me. The purpose of the poster is to highlight the importance of basic digital skills in a way that is easy to read and engaging. The poster will highlight the basic aspects of digital skills, including digital literacy, safe usage, and developing a professional image. It will also highlight how an individual can make use of digital skills, keep their personal details safe, and be aware of cyber threats and develop a positive image. The purpose of the poster is to spread the message of smart and safe usage of technology in today's digital world.
